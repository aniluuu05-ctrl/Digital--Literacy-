##  Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students (India)

###  Actionable Prevention Tips

*   Use strong, unique passwords** for each account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
*   Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on email, banking, and social media accounts
*   Verify links before clicking** – hover over URLs and avoid unknown or shortened links
*   Download apps only from trusted sources** like the Google Play Store or Apple App Store
*   Keep your devices updated** – install security updates for your phone and laptop regularly
*   Limit personal information sharing** on social media (location, phone number, college ID, etc.)
*   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities** like logging into bank accounts

###  UPI & Financial Safety Tips

*  Never share your UPI PIN or OTP** with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank
*  Double-check payment requests** – receiving a “collect request” means *you are paying*, not receiving money
*  Use official apps only** like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm
*  Set transaction limits and alerts** to monitor suspicious activity

###  If Something Goes Wrong

*  Report immediately on the **National Cyber Crime Portal**: cybercrime.gov.in
*  Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** (for quick action on financial fraud)

---

Staying alert and following these simple steps can protect you from most common cyber threats.
