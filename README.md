# Digital--Literacy-

##  Overview

This project focuses on developing awareness about digital literacy, responsible online behavior, and cybersecurity practices. It covers key aspects such as effective digital communication, safe use of social media, understanding cybercrime, and building a professional online presence using platforms like LinkedIn, GitHub, and Kaggle.

## Objectives

* To understand the importance of digital communication
* To promote responsible social media usage
* To create awareness about cybercrime and online safety
* To explore professional platforms for career development

##  Contents of the Project

* Professional Email Writing (Extension request & Internship inquiry)
* Digital Platform Usage (LinkedIn, GitHub, Kaggle)
* Digital Communication Case Study
* Cybercrime Awareness Reflection
* Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
* Project Conclusion

##  Tools Used

* Canva (for design and presentation)
* Google Docs / MS Word (for documentation)
* 

##  Key Learnings

* Importance of clear and professional communication
* Risks associated with cybercrime and preventive measures
* Building a digital portfolio using online platforms
* Responsible and ethical use of social media

##  Safety Awareness

The project highlights the importance of safe online practices such as protecting personal data, avoiding suspicious links, and reporting cybercrime through:

* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline: 1930

## Future Scope

The knowledge gained from this project will help in academic growth, career development, and maintaining a secure digital presence in the future.
