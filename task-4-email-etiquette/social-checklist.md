Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (For College Students)
** Do’s
Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
Think before posting—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal details.
Use social media for learning, networking, and building a positive digital profile.
** Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive information like OTPs, passwords, or bank details.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or harmful comments.
Don’t accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
Don’t click on unknown links or suspicious advertisements.
Don’t overshare personal life details that could affect your safety or reputation.
